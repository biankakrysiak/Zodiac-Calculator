Class Calculate

Needed inputs:
- date and time from JTextFields,
- orbital elements of the planet:
  a = semi-major axis AU
  e = eccentricity
  i = inclination [rad]
  omega = longitude of ascending node [rad]
  w = argument of periapsis [rad]
  M0 = mean anomaly at J2000  [rad]
  P = orbital period [days]
- earth's heliocentric coordinates

Method logic - mathematical chain
1. Calculate JD from date and time
2. Calculate planet's mean anomaly M.
3. Solve Kepler's equasion -> E (eccentric anomaly)
4. Calculate true anomaly v and radius r.
5. Compute orbital plane coordinates [xp, yp, zp].
6. Rotate to heliocentric ecliptic [xh, yh, zh].
7. Calculate Earth's heliocentric coordinates [xE, yE, zE].
8. Subtract Earth's vector -> geocentric vector [xg, yg, xg].
9. Calculate distance delta.
10. Calculate ecliptic longitude lambda and latitude beta.
11. Calculate right ascension alfa and declination beta.
